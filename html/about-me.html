About Me

I Just Wanted To Fix My Laptop...

...and now I'm learning to build websites!

I came across my passion for building things when I decided, enough is enough, I need to fix my laptop.

Whilst troubleshooting for solutions to my problem I realised I enjoyed trying to understand the technology

I had at my finger tips and how it all goes together.

Seeing how simple and yet complex technology is, I decided I wanted to be able to be capable of creating useful things with it.

And what's more useful than websites and applications these days?

Technology is becoming more and more relevant to businesses and consumers.

And so are the people who can create using technology.

A friend of mine who works for Netmatters suggested their Scion scheme to me.

Seeing as he'd already gone through it and had found consistent employment, I thought, why not?

Now here I am building out multiple page websites using HTML, CSS and SASS.
